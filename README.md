# Learnix

Learnix is a playful 3D learning game made in Godot 4.5. Explore a strange digital world, talk to weirdly helpful NPCs, and learn Linux by actually playing instead of staring at a wall of terminal commands.

It is open for everyone who wants to learn Linux through a game, whether you are brand new or just want a lighter way to practice the basics.

## What You Do

- Explore the world and meet characters with something to teach.
- Complete quests and unlock new areas.
- Learn Linux concepts like files, permissions, processes, networking, and system tools.
- Take on quizzes, puzzles, and a few boss-style challenges.


## Controls

- Move: WASD
- Interact: LMB / E
- Dependency Minigame: Down key ⬇️ to change from Drag Mode to Connect Mode, Left ⬅️ / Right ➡️ Key to Select Nodes, LMB to place Nodes, and C to reset the App Goal
- Timing Minigame: Spacebar
- Follow quests: Check your objectives and talk to NPCs
